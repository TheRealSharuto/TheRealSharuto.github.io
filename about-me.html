Shara Belton


In 2019, I graduated two years early with a Kinesiology degree, believing my path was forever routed in the health and wellness industry. However, I only needed half a year as a Medical Assistant to know the becoming a medical professional was not for me.

I had entered a crisis. Graduating right after I had turned 20 left me with a deep misunderstanding of what I truly want in life. There was something BIG missing in my career path - or any path I could take with a Kinesiology degree for that matter. That BIG thing turned out to be creativity. In other words, every bone in my body was made to create things - and my eyes were made to see the results of said creatons. Without this aspect, I am not fulfilled.

Soon, I realized that all my favorite things - gaming, video editing, animation, and more - all had a foundation and origination of code.

I decided that...I might as well learn how to code! Originally I felt that I would not go back to school - so I hunkered down and researched every free source possible and began with HTML & CSS. Silly me! I thought I would end up hating code. However, my whole life I was addicted to being on my computer to create things like videos, meme edits, stories, and designs. It was only a matter of time that I would get addicted to code.

Amazingly, I left the health industry to become an Administrative Assistant. Today, I am looking for new work, self-teaching code, have created a blog, will begin a Web Development bootcamp with Generation USA, and will be returning to school for my Computer Science Bachelor's Degree.

Follow me in my journey on Shara's Bubbles to keep up with what I learn each week. I will also be posting information and advice regarding tech and productivity on a weekly basis.
