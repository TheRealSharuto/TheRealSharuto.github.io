Shara Belton


In 2019, I graduated two years early with a Kinesiology degree, believing my path was forever routed in the health and wellness industry. However, I only needed half a year as a Medical Assistant to know the becoming a medical professional was not for me.

I had entered a crisis. Graduating right after I had turned 20 left me with a deep misunderstanding of what I truly want in life. There was something BIG missing in my career path - or any path I could take with a Kinesiology degree for that matter. That BIG thing turned out to be creativity. In other words, every bone in my body was made to create things - and my eyes were made to see the results of said creatons. Without this aspect, I am not fulfilled.

At the time, I was obssessed with cryptocurrency. I had to read all about it and figure out just how exactly blockchains work. It was then that I realized that all my favorite things - cryptocurrency, gaming, video editing, animation, and more - all had a foundation and origination of code.

I decided that...I mine as well learn how to code! I could not afford to go back to school though - so I hunkered down and researched every free source possible and began with HTML & CSS. Silly me! I thought I would end up hating code. However, my whole life I was addicted to being on my computer to create things like videos, meme edits, stories, and designs. It was only a matter of time that I would get addicted to code.

Amazingly, I left the health industry. During the day I am an Administrative Assistant to pay the bills. But, at night, my Hannah Montana side comes out, performing as a front-end/UX developer!

Now? My passion lies in both coding and designing for a all-inclusive, stress free user experience that always remains aesthetically pleasing, functional, and responsive.
